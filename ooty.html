THE QUEEN OF HILLS


Ootythe Queen of hill stations it is fully covered with greenery and with mild climate which mesmerise the tourist.The best time to visit Ooty is throughout the year as the weather is pleasant and great for sightseeing. The temperature usually ranges from 5-15 degrees throughout the year with colder nights.

Some of the best spots to visit in ooty are:

  1. Botanical garden

  2. Needle view hillpoint

  3. Dolphin's nose

  4. Ooty lake

  5. Nilgiri Mountain Railway
